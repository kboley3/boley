Mini Bio

I grew up in a rural part of northwest Georgia where I spent a lot of time outside gazing at the stars. For my first few years of college, I attended Georgia Highlands College where I received my Associates in Physics. During this time, I worked at various jobs, but my absolute favorite was working as a dance instructor at Baird Ballet. Later, I moved to Atlanta to attend Georgia Institute of Technology. While there, I became involved with their Army ROTC program where I earned an educational scholarship. Upon receiving my B.S. in Physics, I commissioned as a Second Lieutenant in the U.S. Army reserves. Currently, I am serving as a Platoon Leader in a ground ambulance unit.

Hobbies

When I'm not doing research, I absolutely love being active and exploring. I am an avid scuba diver, and dive as often as I can. It is amazing to explore in lake and oceans. I am very active with the Columbus Scuba Club. Currently, I'm certified as a rescue diver, and I'm working on my Master Diver certification. I also enjoy hobbies on land as well. Hiking and snowboarding are two other hobbies that I love. One of my favorite places that I've hiked to is Kling King Beach on Nusa Penida.
