About Me

I am an astronomy Ph.D student at The Ohio State University. My current project is working on detecting terristrial planets orbiting Halo stars with Ji Wang as my advisor. Previously, I worked with Will Dawson on faint galaxy detection of highly sheared galaxies at Lawrence Livermore National Laboratory. I am also a second lieutenant in the Army Reserves, which has allowed me to develop my leadership skills.
